Video generation: WanVideo or LTXVideo?
Use Wan when you want the most common native ComfyUI video route and broader T2V/I2V workflow coverage; official Wan2.1/2.2 workflows are documented and Wan2.2 includes 5B and 14B routes. Use LTXVideo / LTX-2 when you specifically want LTX’s newer audio-video / advanced video features; LTX-2 is built into ComfyUI core, with the custom LTXVideo repo adding advanced nodes/workflows.
