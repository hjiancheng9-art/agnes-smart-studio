# Doubt-Driven Development
## Description
Subject every non-trivial decision to adversarial verification.
## Instructions
1. For every design choice, ask: what is the strongest argument AGAINST this?
2. Find at least one alternative approach and compare objectively
3. Test the alternative with a quick prototype before committing
4. Document why the chosen approach won over alternatives
5. Revisit decisions when new evidence emerges
6. If you cannot articulate why choice A beats B, you are not ready to decide
